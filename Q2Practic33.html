The Fragrant Flower Blooms with Dignity /p>


Synopsis:


The Fragrant Flower Blooms with Dignity is a romantic comedy manga and anime series about Rintaro Tsumugi, a kind-hearted boy with an intimidating face who attends a public high school for delinquents, and Kaoruko Waguri, a gentle girl from a wealthy, elite all-girls school located next door.
